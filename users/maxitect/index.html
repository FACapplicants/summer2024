About me

Hi! I'm Max, I'm an architect and computational designer currently working in the AEC* industry. I have a background in programming in Python, C# and data analytics but very little web development experience.

My experience

My specialism is computational design. Computational design uses a rules-based approach and algorithmic design to solve building engineering design problems. This can take the form of designing automated design workflows for multiple projects or parametric workflows to model and resolve designs involving complex geometries. I have worked alongside front end, back end and data engineers to deliver bespoke apps and workflows for clients in the AEC* industry.
